## Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

**Day 1 – Seattle:** Pike Place Market → Waterfront → Seattle Aquarium → Olympic Sculpture Park → Space Needle & Chihuly → Capitol Hill and Starbucks Reserve.

**Day 2 – Mountains:** Snoqualmie Falls → Mount Rainier National Park (or Rattlesnake Ledge if you prefer a shorter day) → Dinner in North Bend or Issaquah.

**Day 3 – Puget Sound:** Morning ferry to Bainbridge Island → Lunch in Winslow → Return to Seattle via ferry → Ballard Locks and Golden Gardens for sunset (or substitute Deception Pass if they're willing to spend more time driving).

This combination gives visitors a balanced introduction to the Pacific Northwest's defining landscapes—volcanic mountains, evergreen forests, Puget Sound ferries, and Seattle's waterfront culture.
